Photography


Photo is created in accordance with the vision of the photographer as artist. Photo is to express the photographer's perceptions and emotions and to share them with others. People prefer to see things with their own eyes, but when this is impossible the camera can often serve the same purpose almost as well… As a nonverbal means of communication, photography can surmount the barriers of language and communicate through universal visual symbols.

A camera, no matter how many automatic features it may have, is a lifeless piece of equipment until a person uses it. It then becomes a uniquely responsive tool--an extension of the photographer's eye and mind. Today photography is widely recognized as a fine art. Photographs are displayed in art museums, prized by collectors, discussed by critics, and studied in art history courses.
