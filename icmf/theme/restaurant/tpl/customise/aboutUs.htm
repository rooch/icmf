OUR STORY


The BEGINING.....

AranBia Pizza Trading as Queen Margherita

We started humbly but with a grand plan: To create the finest pizza this world has ever tasted,End of story. And with nothing but a food cart and this dream, we've grown and grown and exploded to this point where you're reading this right now and probably salivating. Our secret? It's not one, admittedly. The best ingredients is a big part. An unrelenting love for the pizza is the other big part. Between those two, we think we pretty much have our pizza bases covered. Turns out we also like people, though. We're not making these delicious, one-of-a-kind pizza creations to not share them with our human species brethren. We hope to share them with you, in fact. And soon. So head on in to the nearest (heck, it could even be the furthest) stockist and feast your eyes on the greatest bread ever made. You won't be sorry you did.
